About Crystal
"Technology is just a tool.
Trust is the infrastructure."

Crystal Pugh is a Brooklyn-based cybersecurity strategist, Virtual CTO, and AI advisor with over 15 years spanning technology consulting, organizational change management, and learning & development. She founded Cyber Protection Consulting Services Inc. (CPI) to give nonprofits, social enterprises, and mission-driven organizations access to the kind of strategic technology leadership that usually only comes with a Fortune 500 budget.

Her work is grounded in a simple belief: that trust is not a feature you add at the end of a project. It is the architecture. That conviction shapes every engagement — from govcon compliance strategy to HIPAA-compliant AI builds to board-level technology governance.
